About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Margaret Wangari, a web developer and designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I specialize in creating beautiful, functional websites that help businesses and individuals establish their online presence.

With a background in Computer Science and a passion for aesthetics, I bridge the gap between technical functionality and stunning visual design. I believe that the best websites are those that not only look great but also provide an intuitive user experience.

When I'm not coding or designing, I love to cook, explore nature trails, and read contemporary fiction. I'm also an amateur photographer who enjoys capturing moments that tell stories.
